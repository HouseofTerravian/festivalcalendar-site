Our Story

About FestivalCalendar.online

Building the easiest place on the internet to discover, save, and experience festivals.

The Mission

Festival discovery is broken. Event information is scattered across city websites, Facebook groups, ticketing platforms, tourism boards, and outdated calendars. People who want to attend music festivals, food festivals, art shows, wellness retreats, and cultural celebrations have to hunt across a dozen different sources to find what's near them.

FestivalCalendar.online centralizes festival discovery into one clean, searchable, SEO-friendly platform. We believe anyone should be able to find a great festival near them in under a minute — no hunting required.

What We're Building

1

Public Discovery

A searchable, always-free public festival directory organized by city, state, date, weekend, and category.

2

Personal Calendar

Sign in with Noo World to save festivals, follow cities, and build a personal festival calendar that remembers your preferences.

3

Organizer Tools

Simple, affordable tools for festival organizers and vendors to reach the right audiences at the right time.

For Festival-Goers

Whether you're a music fan hunting for your next big concert weekend, a foodie searching for the best culinary events in your city, a wellness seeker looking for yoga retreats, or a family wanting to find local cultural celebrations — FestivalCalendar.online has one goal: help you find your next great experience faster.

For Organizers & Vendors

Festival organizers deserve affordable, effective ways to reach people actively looking for events. Vendors deserve a place to promote their presence and connect with organizers. We built FestivalCalendar.online to serve both sides of that relationship.

Free listings let any organizer get discovered. Featured placements give those who want more visibility a lightweight, non-bloated path to reach more people.

Powered by Noo World

FestivalCalendar.online is a discovery platform within the Noo World ecosystem. The personal festival calendar, saved events, city follows, and weekly alerts are powered by Noo World ID — a single identity layer that connects users across multiple discovery platforms.

You don't need a Noo World account to browse festivals. But if you want to save your favorites, follow cities, and receive personalized weekly alerts, it's free to create one.

We're Early — And Growing

FestivalCalendar.online launched in 2026. We're building our festival database, expanding city coverage, and developing the full organizer dashboard, vendor marketplace, and personalized alert system.

If you have a festival to submit, a vendor business to promote, or ideas for how we can improve discovery — we want to hear from you.
